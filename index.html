Style Guide

Every Major Tattoo Style

Not sure what style is right for you? Here's the breakdown.

🌹 American Traditional

Bold black outlines, limited color palette, iconic imagery. Roses, eagles, anchors, ships.

🎭 Neo-Traditional

Like Traditional but with more detail, depth, and expanded color. Surrealist and illustrative elements.

⛩️ Japanese / Irezumi

Large-scale storytelling. Dragons, koi, cherry blossoms. Designed to flow with the body.

◼ Blackwork

Bold black ink only. Geometric, tribal, illustrative. High contrast, graphic design aesthetic.

◈ Geometric

Precise line work, mathematical shapes, sacred geometry. Often combined with other styles.

🖊️ Fine Line / Minimalist

Single-needle delicate work. Small, subtle, precise. Popular for first tattoos.
